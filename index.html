About

I’m Veronica - with 15 years experience as a senior corporate executive in the Insurance sector specialising in Sales and Distribution. I find myself engulfed in the world of Data Analytics, where I'm teaching myself and exploring new tools and methods. I have a foundation in Economics & Finance (University of Bristol, UK) and professional training as a Chartered Accountant (ACCA).

I'm also satisfying my curiosity with human behaviour where I'm in the midst of completing a Post Graduate Diploma in Psychology with UTS. If you can't already tell, I’m obsessed with the intersection of data, decision-making, and human behaviour!

My work is about turning noise into signal — building dashboards, making connections across businesses, and unravelling the complex to make it feel simple.
