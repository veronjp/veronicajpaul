About

I’m Veronica — a self-taught data analyst with a foundation in Economics & Finance (University of Bristol, UK) and professional training as a Chartered Accountant (ACCA).

I’ve worked across insurance/financial services and more recently FMCG, and I’m currently completing a Post Graduate Diploma in Psychology. I’m obsessed with the intersection of data, decision-making, and human behaviour.

My work is about turning noise into signal — building dashboards, analysing customer journeys, and writing explainers that make complex things feel simple.
